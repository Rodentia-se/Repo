    I (Christian) am currently 25 years old, 1.89m (about 6'3") male with light blond hair. I have a sister called Lotta who is two years elder than me. She is working at a place called DotCom and lives together with Daniel. Lotta and Daniel lives in an appartment on Stora Essingen in Stockholm. My parents (Bo and Gunilla) still live in Kristianstad where I stayed until 1990. In the summer they usually spend their time in Åhus (about 20km from Kristianstad). Åhus is famous for Abolut Vodka and is a very nice crowded place in the summer. There they play golf and take care of our summer house.

    Most of my old friends from Kristianstad has moved away, but there are still some of them living there. One of those is Christian Andersson (and his brother), who I spend some time together with.

    I like holidays very much and try to go somewhere outside sweden every year to relax. My uncle Ulf lives in Spain now, and one of my aunt Inga-Maj lives in Italy (Rom) and has a summer house in Greece (Korfu), so those are places that I try to visit if I have some spare time.

    One of my hobbies is golf. I have currently Hcp 5.6, but I'm only playing some months in the summer... mostly on Kristianstad Golf Course.

    Cooking is one of my favourite too. During my studies at Chalmers, I've tried many new receips with my friend Malaga. (We still haven't agreed on which frying-pan is the best, and how should rice be cooked... :-)

    I've also spent some time in front of computers during my youth. This is the computers I've owned:

  • 1980 I rent my first VIC-20.
  • 1982 It didn't take long time before I bought my first computer. This was a Commodore 64 with a 1541 disk drive.
  • 1983-1985 I worked in a computer shop after school.
  • 1986 I bought an Amiga 2000. I really enjoyed this machine, and I learned to program some C and made my first serious programs.
  • 1993 I decided to buy an Amiga 4000/040, 14Mb RAM, 1.5Gb HD.
  • For the moment I have a Pentium Pro200 64Mb EDO RAM, 5Gb HD, 8x CD-ROM, SoundBlaster 32 Vibra, Diamond Stealth 64 Video 4Mb and 10Mbit ethernetcard. I'm currently running Linux at my computer.
