General Information

Netherland won the team competition 1996, and they were offered to organize the 3rd European Pinball Championship. They accepted the offer and have already planned to run the competition in:
Place:
MAASTRICHT/Valkenburg, Netherland
Date:
Nov 1st-2nd 1997, (Saturday - Sunday)
Maastricht is located in the most southern part of the country near the Belgium en German border.

If you want some more information you should get in contact with the Dutch Pinball Association.
