Travel - How to get there

 

The location is about 10 km away from the airport, by public transport less than 30 minutes:

Zurich Airport to Zentrum Glatt: Bus NFB, 759 direction Dübendorf

Zentrum Glatt to Hörnligraben: Bus 787, direction Brüttisellen

If you travel by car, there's almost no parking in the surrounding area!
