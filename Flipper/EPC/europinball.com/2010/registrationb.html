Registration

How registration works

- Step 1: read the registration and payment details
- Step 2: download the registration form
- Step 3: fill in the registration form (one per participant)
- Step 4: send the registration form(s) to 2010europinball@gmail.com
- Step 5: you will get a confirmation of receipt (this confirmation does not empower to participate!)
- Step 6: we will send you details of how and where to pay for your entry or you will be put on the reserve list if all places are full
- Step 7: pay within 14 days or your spot will go to the first player on the reserve list
- Step 8: you will get a confirmation and you're registered

Registration details

- Registration is open from 03.05-28.05.2010
- The registration fee is 44€/65 CHF payable in advance
- There are 160 slots available for the Main Tournament
- If a country takes part in the Country Competition, 6 slots are guaranteed; if not, 2 slots are guaranteed per country
- If there are more than 160 registrations, we will use the World Pinball Player Rankings (standings from the 28th of May 2010) as a contributing factor
- you can get a 2€/3CHF benefit by pre-order your meal (only a very, very small amount of meals will be available at the desk!)

Payment details

There are three methods of payment:

1.: Bank Transfer within Switzerland (CHF/€)
(add 3CHF/2€ by paying at the postal-office counter)

2.: International Bank Transfer (only €)
(Please make sure, that surcharges are covered - if not, the difference is payable on spot)

3.: PayPal (only CHF)
(for residents outside the Single Euro Payment Area - SEPA or if your bank invoices high charges; add 3.5% to the total amount)
