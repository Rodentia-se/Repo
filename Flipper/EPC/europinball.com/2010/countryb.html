Qualification

The Teams will be divided into two groups. Within each group, there will be matches against all other teams in this group in which two teams play each other on two machines, with two players from each team playing a four player, three ball game. Points are awarded as follows:

1st - 9 points
2nd - 5 points
3rd - 2 points
4th - zero points

The three teams with the highest points from each group will go on to the finals. In the event that more than two teams are tied on the qualifying bubble, such that not all of the tied teams can advance to the final rounds, a tiebreaking procedure will be utilized.

Finals

Same rules as in the qualification. In case of a tie there will be a single game on a single machine in which all eight players of both teams participate. The machine will be set up for 4 balls per game and a 'two player game' will be started; each player of each team play one single ball in this game.
