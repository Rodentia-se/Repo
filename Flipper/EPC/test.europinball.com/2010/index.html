About the venue

The EPC 2010 will be held at three different rooms (they are all at the same place!).
On location, only Swiss Francs are accepted! (or 5€=6CHF)

 
Hurricane Flipperclub
Basement - Main Tournament, 10-15 recreational machines (1 Hurri-Jeton, 5 Balls), Bar, Snacks
Due to fire authority the admission is limited
Rules of the house (only in german)

Geraldo's
Ground Floor right-hand - Country Competition and Classics Tournament

Paninoteca da Valentino
Ground Floor left-hand - Set the Highscore and catering
The Paninoteca is an independent partner. Please do not bring your own food and beverages in it and regard the different opening hours!

Smoking is prohibeted by law in the inside. In front of the building there will be a counter with some seat-accomodations. 

About the catering

Our partner, Paninoteca da Valentino, cares about the meals on Saturday evening and Sunday noon. Because we have to pre-order them, only a very small amount will be available at the desk or at the Paninoteca itself. We can offer you a 2€/3CHF discount per meal by advance order (transferable).
Beverages will be available at the Paninoteca, Hurricane and the counter outside.

Note that there are almost no shops or restaurants at walking distance!
