Set the Highscore

Date: 15th to 17th October

Location: Paninoteca da Valentino

 

Rules

- Costs per game are 2CHF or 2 Hurri-Jetons (=2CHF).

- Play as often as you want!

- The best player on each of the 2-3 machines gets 25% of the whole jackpot, consisting of the earnings from the 2-3 machines.

- Highscores will be identified by the players' TAG, so pay attention by entering your initials. 

Please respect Paninoteca's opening hours, there are not the same as the Hurricane's.
